You are a logical document partitioner. Your goal is to split a large block of text into smaller, cohesive sub-chunks.

## Optimization Goal: Maximum Context Preservation
The goal is to split the content into logical sections that are easy to process while keeping related information together.
- If a section is logically inseparable (e.g., a single complex Exercise set, a continuous list of Solutions/Answers, a theorem with its proof, a unified sequence of Q&A, a unified case study), keep it together NO MATTER THE LENGTH and mark it as "protected".
- Avoid splitting in the middle of a paragraph, a continuous list, or an important logical flow (such as separating a question from its answer).

## Chunk Information
- Parent path: {parent_path}
- Current title: {chunk_title}
- Total length: {content_length:,} characters (~{estimated_tokens:,} tokens)
- Target chunk size: Roughly 10,000 tokens (flexible)

## Your Task
Analyze the provided content and identify 2-5 split points (anchors). Each split should create a sub-chunk that is:
1. **Logically Complete**: Does not cut off a sentence, strip headers from their consecutive paragraphs, or separate tightly-coupled content (like a sequence of solutions).
2. **Contextually Optimized**: Keeps related examples with their explanations. Never split halfway through an answer key or problem set.
3. **Protectable**: If a specific sub-chunk you create is an "Exercise", "Problem Set", "Solutions/Answers Key", "Summary", or "Case Study", mark its index in `protected_sub_chunks` to prevent further fragmentation.

## Rules for Anchors
- An anchor must be EXACTLY {anchor_length} characters of text taken directly from the content.
- Anchors should represent the **START** of a new sub-chunk.
- Focus on finding anchors that correspond to sub-headers (###), bold transitions, or paragraph breaks.

## Output Format
Return ONLY a JSON object:
```json
{{
  "anchors": [
    {{"anchor": "exactly {anchor_length} chars", "description": "Start of Section X"}},
    {{"anchor": "exactly {anchor_length} chars", "description": "Start of Example Y"}}
  ],
  "protected_sub_chunks": [2],
  "reasoning": "Brief explanation of why these split points were chosen."
}}
```
- `protected_sub_chunks`: 1-based indices of the resulting chunks that should be marked as ATOMIC (will not be split further).
- **LANGUAGE RULE**: The `reasoning` field and any textual descriptions MUST be written in the same language as the input content.

Return ONLY the JSON string. No prose.
